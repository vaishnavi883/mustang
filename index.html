HEAD OF OPERATIONS

Our Head of Department (HoD) for the Oil & Gas Division is a highly experienced professional with deep expertise in energy technologies and process engineering. With a strong academic background and decades of industry experience, the HoD leads a dynamic team focused on innovation, sustainability, and efficiency in gas purification and biofuel systems.

Under their leadership, the department has developed cutting-edge Methyl Amine-based purification systems for bio-gas, introduced agricultural crop residue-to-bio-gas conversion technologies, and achieved 99.99% gas purity through advanced packing and demister systems.

Integrated data-driven design with predictive maintenance alerts. Their vision, technical knowledge, and commitment to excellence continue to drive the department toward making impactful contributions to clean and sustainable energy solutions.
